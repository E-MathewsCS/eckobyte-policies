1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

HomeVault is a local-first application. All inventory data you enter — including item names, descriptions, values, photos, serial numbers, warranty dates, beneficiary information, and any other fields — is stored exclusively on your device in a local database. We do not operate servers that store your personal inventory data. We do not have access to your inventory, photos, or personal information.

1.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous Identifiers: RevenueCat (our subscription management provider) generates an anonymous user ID to manage purchases and entitlements. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Purchase Records: When you make an in-app purchase, Google Play and RevenueCat process and store transaction records in accordance with their respective privacy policies.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Google Firebase to collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g., screen views, feature usage, crash reports). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.
  • AI Smart Scan: If you use the AI-powered Smart Scan feature, images are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Google processes these images in accordance with their Cloud AI terms of service. We do not store or retain any images on our servers.

1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Contacts, call logs, or messages
  • Browsing history
  • Financial account numbers, credit card numbers, or bank details
  • Health or biometric data

2. How We Use Information

  • To improve the App through anonymous, aggregated analytics
  • To provide AI-powered item identification (when you opt into Smart Scan)

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Photos and attachments are stored in your device's app-private storage directory.
  • Backup files (ZIP exports) are created on your device and shared only when you explicitly choose to share them.
  • We do not transmit your inventory data to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and backup files.

6. Data Retention & Deletion
